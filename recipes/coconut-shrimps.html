Coconut Shrimps

Description

These coconut shrimp are rolled in a coconut beer batter before frying. Serve warm with your favorite dipping sauce â€” I use orange marmalade, mustard, and horseradish mixed to taste for a dipping sauce.

Ingredients

  • All purpose flour
  • Egg
  • Beer
  • Baking Powder
  • Shrimps
  • Unsweetened Coconut Flakes
  • Vegetable Oil

Steps

  1. Whisk some of the flour, the egg, beer, and baking powder together.
  2. Dredge the shrimp in flour, dip it in the beer batter, and roll in the coconut flakes.
  3. Refrigerate the shrimp for half an hour, then fry the shrimp in hot oil until golden brown.
